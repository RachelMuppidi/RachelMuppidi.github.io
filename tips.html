1. If your brace starts to hurt the first few days you wear it, there is no need to worry! It takes time for your body to adjust to the brace. Make sure that you get a lot rest so that you do not feel as much pain or discomfort.

2. If you need more Diamond Bands, you can visit the "Contact Us" tab and order extra brace bands from the company.

3. Try to keep the brace on for at least ten hours a day. This will help minimize the amount of time you spend in the brace.

4. Make sure you remember to visit your scoliosis doctor one every six months. This will help your doctor track your back progression.

5. Always remember that you are strong and brave! Do not be embarrassed to wear your brace to school or other events.

6. Try not to lie on your stomach since it can cause your thoracic spine to become worse.

7. Do not carry heavy objects for a long period of time, as the extended pressure can cause more back and muscle pain.

8. Finding the right matress can help ease the discomfort. Usually, a firm or meduim-firm mattress works the best.

9. Stretching can help ease the pain and discomfort. You can perform five sets of fifteen repetitions of chest stretches each day.

10. If your back starts to ache during the night, you can support your spine with small pillows or rolled-up towels.

11. If your back is constantly bothering you, never wait to schedule an appointment. Instead, immediately set up an appointment with your doctor.

12. Do not only stretch, but try working out as well.

13. Wear comfortable shoes such as sneakers; do not wear flip flops or heals.

14. Keep an eye on your posture. Always make sure that you are keeps your back straight.

15. Perform yoga in order to reduce the angle of your back due to scoliosis.

16. Try biofeedback, which is a treatment method where you become aware of your body's reactions and learn to control them through your actions.

17. Drink lots of water each day.

18. When driving, do not sit too far back. Reaching for the pedals or steering wheel strains the back, and stretching out the arms tires the upper back.

19. If you stand a lot, use a foot stool or a step on which to rest one foot. This will ultimately take the strain off the lower back.

20. Make friends that support both you and your decisions.

21. You make visit a chiropractor if you need to decrease pain or to improve posture.

22. Stand up and stretch as often as possible.

23. If you are experiencing painful muscle spasms, apply a hot pack to your back for 20 to 30 minutes.

24. Do not feel afraid or embarrassed to wear your brace. Instead, celebrate the fact that you are changing for the better!

25. You may want to participate in certain sports, including soccer and swimming, in order to improve the curve of your spine.

26. Try investing in a matress topper, however make sure it is not too thick since it will not give your neck and spine the support it needs (Buy a mattress topper that is 1-3 inches thick).

27. If you are worried about your appearance, you can wear off-the-shoulder shirts to drawa attention away from uneven shoulders and hips.

28. Layer your clothes if you do not want the outline of your brace to show. You can wear blazers, jean jackets, or cardigans.

29. High-waisted jeans are great for hiding your curve and complimenting your posture.

30. You may wear a swing dress since they are not very fitted and are comfortable.

31. Sometimes, it may hurt for you to sit down while working. If this is the case, you can try investing in a standing desk.
